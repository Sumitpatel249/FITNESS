🔍 Diet Weaknesses
Kya thoda better ho sakta hai?
🥩
Protein Bahut Low Hai
6-day workout + night shift ke liye 60-70g protein chahiye. Abhi sirf ~28g milti hai. Har meal mein dal/egg/chana add karein.
🌿
Vegetables Almost Absent
Vitamins + fiber ke liye daily 2-3 servings sabzi zaroori hai. Spinach, lauki, tinda, cucumber — sab saste aur healthy hain.
⚡
Calorie Distribution Uneven
Post-workout meal bahut chhota hai, main meal late evening hai. Poor calorie distribution = slow metabolism + fatigue.
⏰
Long Fasting Window
Wake (4 PM) se main meal (6 PM) = 2 ghante, phir 6 PM se raat 2-3 AM tak kuch nahi. 8-9 ghante ka fasting too long for night shift.

✅ Kya Acha Hai
• Chia seeds — omega-3, fiber, calcium
• Lemon water — Vitamin C, hydration
• Boiled egg — quality protein, choline
• Dal roti — complex carbs + protein combo
• Budget conscious approach — sustainable
• Sleep timing fixed — circadian support
⚠️ Kya Theek Karna Hai
• Sirf 1 egg post-workout — protein too low
• No vegetables in current plan
• No fruit mentioned except possibly banana
• Night shift mein no planned meals
• Iron + Calcium sources missing
• Calorie gap between meals too large
💪 Fat Loss + Toning Ke Liye Kya?
Current diet is calorie-moderate but protein deficient. For toning: aapko protein 50-60g tak laana hai — affordable way: 2 eggs + dal + moong/chana. Fat loss ke liye overall deficit achha hai lekin muscle loss risk bhi hai agar protein low rahe.
🌙 Night Shift Pe Impact
Night shift body clock alter karta hai — metabolism slow, hunger hormones off-cycle. Solution: har 3-4 ghante mein kuch chhota khaana (khajoor, chana, banana) aur 200ml paani regular. Isse fatigue less hogi aur weight management bhi better rahega.
